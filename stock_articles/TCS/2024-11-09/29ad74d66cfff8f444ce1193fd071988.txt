Tata Consultancy Services (TCS), a leading IT software company, has been making headlines with its recent stock performance. On November 6, 2024, the company's stock gained 3.13%, closing at Rs 4095. This positive movement has caught the attention of investors and market experts, with MarketsMOJO giving a 'Buy' call for the stock.

In terms of price performance, TCS has been inline with the IT software sector, which has seen a gain of 2.56%. The stock has also been on a consecutive upward trend for the past 2 days, with a 2.95% increase in returns during this period. The day's high for TCS was Rs 4100, a 3.17% increase from the previous day.

When compared to the moving averages, TCS is currently higher than the 5-day and 200-day moving averages, but lower than the 20-day, 50-day, and 100-day moving averages. This indicates a positive trend for the stock, with potential for further growth.
